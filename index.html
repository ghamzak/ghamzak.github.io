Website last updated on 10/02/2023.

Enterprise AI: How can we enable machines to learn, understand, and interact in a human way, to meet organizational needs?

I believe 2023 can be labeled as the year when a "revolution" happened in AI, when a new era of AI started. Numerous startups were founded just to harness the new capabilities offered by AI models. With a PhD in "natural language processing", which is a subset of AI, and having first-hand experience with developing and using these foundation models, I am convinced that AI has and can further make a significant difference in people's quality of life. I enjoy and am excited about being a part of this revolution.
During my PhD at University of Colorado Boulder, I was fortunate enough to have the great Martha Palmer as my PhD advisor. Her interdisciplinary perspective, and her flexibility gave me a great opportunity to learn, grow, and challenge myself with what I really enjoy.

Machine Learning · Deep Learning · Natural Language Underdtanding · Semantic Search · Machine Reading Comprehension · Generative AI
